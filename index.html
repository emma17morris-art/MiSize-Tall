üì± How to Use MiSize - Complete Guide

Step-by-step instructions for using all features

1Getting Started

Welcome to MiSize! Start by selecting your region (UK, EU, US, CA) from the home screen to access region-specific sizing and brands.

üí° Tip: Choose the region where you shop most frequently for the most accurate sizing information.

2Setting Your Body Type & Units

At the top of your screen, you'll see two pill groups:

  • Body Type: Select Standard, Petite, Tall, or Plus to filter results for your body type
  • Units: Choose between cm or inches for measurements
3Using Size Finder

Find your perfect size across different brands:

  • Select a brand from our extensive list of 20+ popular retailers
  • Choose your category (Jeans, Dresses, Tops, Shoes)
  • Pick your fit preference (Standard, Snug, or Relaxed)
  • Tap "Get My Size" to see your recommended size
  • Use "Save to History" to keep track of your searches

üîç Pro Tip: The app considers your body type and measurements to provide the most accurate size recommendations.

4My Sizes Dashboard

View all your sizes in one place:

  • Automatically filtered by your selected body type
  • Only shows brands that support your body type
  • Filter by category to find specific items quickly
  • See your recommended size for each brand and category
