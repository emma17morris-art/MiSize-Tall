Brand Notes

Information about UK brands and their sizing

ASOS

Categories: Women's Jeans, Dresses, Tops, Shoes

Body Types Supported: Standard, Petite, Tall, Plus

Sizing Notes: Generally runs true to size. Petite range available for heights under 5'3". Tall range for 5'8"+. Plus sizes 16-30.

Fit Guide: Size down for slim fit jeans, true to size for regular fit.

Zara

Categories: Women's Jeans, Dresses, Tops

Body Types Supported: Standard, Tall

Sizing Notes: Tends to run small. Consider sizing up. Limited petite options. TRF line runs smaller than main line.

Fit Guide: Size up for most items, especially jackets and structured pieces.

H&M

Categories: Women's Jeans, Dresses, Tops

Body Types Supported: Standard, Plus

Sizing Notes: Sizing varies by line. Conscious collection runs larger. Plus sizes available up to 24.

Fit Guide: Check individual item reviews as sizing is inconsistent across collections.
