üì± How to Use MiSize - Complete Guide

1Getting Started

Welcome to MiSize! Start by selecting your region (UK, EU, US, CA) from the home screen to access region-specific sizing and brands.

üí° Tip: Choose the region where you shop most frequently for the most accurate sizing information.

2Setting Your Body Type & Units

At the top of your screen, you'll see two pill groups:

  • Body Type: Select Standard, Petite, Tall, or Plus to filter results for your body type
  • Units: Choose between cm or inches for measurements
3Using Size Finder

Find your perfect size across different brands:

  • Select a brand from our extensive list of 20+ popular retailers
  • Choose your category (Jeans, Dresses, Tops, Shoes)
  • Pick your fit preference (Standard, Snug, or Relaxed)
  • Tap "Get My Size" to see your recommended size
  • Use "Save to History" to keep track of your searches

üîç Pro Tip: The app considers your body type and measurements to provide the most accurate size recommendations.

4My Sizes Dashboard

View all your sizes in one place:

  • Automatically filtered by your selected body type
  • Only shows brands that support your body type
  • Filter by category to find specific items quickly
  • See your recommended size for each brand and category
5Brand Notes

Access detailed information about each brand:

  • Sizing systems used (UK, EU, US)
  • Which body types they support
  • Available categories and product ranges
  • Special sizing notes and fit recommendations
6Size Converters

Convert sizes between different regions:

  • Dress Sizes: UK ‚Üî US ‚Üî EU ‚Üî CA conversion
  • Jean Sizes: Convert between different sizing systems
  • Top Sizes: Blouses, shirts, and casual tops
  • Shoe Sizes: International shoe size conversion
  • Bra Helper: Find your perfect bra size across regions
7MiStats Body Tracker

Track your body measurements and progress:

Current Progress Tab:
  • View your starting, current, and target measurements
  • Track progress with visual progress bars
  • Monitor body fat percentage, weight lost, and remaining goals
  • Keep an eye on your BMI status
Graph Tab:
  • Visualize your progress over time
  • Switch between weekly, monthly, and yearly views
  • Zoom into specific time periods
  • Toggle between graph and table view
History Tab:
  • Complete log of all your recorded measurements
  • Organized by date for easy tracking
  • Include weight, body measurements, and sizing data
8Navigation Tips

Getting around the app efficiently:

  • Bottom Navigation: Use Home, MiStats, Settings, Help, and Back buttons
  • Back Button: Returns you to your previous screen
  • Home Button: Always takes you to the main region selection
  • Regional Hubs: Each region has its own specialized tools and data

‚ö° Quick Tip: You can switch between body types and units at any time using the top bar controls - your preferences are saved automatically!

9Best Practices

Get the most out of MiSize:

  • Update your measurements regularly for accurate sizing
  • Save interesting size searches to build your personal database
  • Check brand notes before shopping for sizing insights
  • Use converters when shopping internationally
  • Set realistic goals in your body tracker
10Troubleshooting

Common questions and solutions:

  • Size seems wrong? Check your body type setting and brand notes
  • Missing measurements? Use our measurement guides in the Help section
  • Can't find a brand? Check if you're in the right regional hub
  • Progress not showing? Ensure you're logging measurements regularly
